Angelo's recipe

Migliotto Ã  la Angelo

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin, 800 gr.
  • Lentils, 800 gr.
  • Millet, 200gr.
  • Celery, 1 qt
  • Shallot, 1 qt
  • Vegetable sauce, 1 lt

Preparation

  1. Cut all vegetables and let them cook for 15 min at low temperature.
  2. Add lentils and pumpkin, let them cook per 15 min at low temperature.
  3. Add millet. Mix with the sauce, as much as needed for it to be well cooked (average 20 min).
